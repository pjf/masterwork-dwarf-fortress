Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get scalps?

Scalps are automatically harvested when you butcher the corpses of worthy enemies ("Civilisations" in Masterwork Dwarf Fortress). However automatons drop gears instead of scalps, and frost giants drop bifrost instead.

Tanning a scalp is an automatic reaction that occurs at the tannery. You may wish to build extra tanning workshops if you're butchering many enemies.

How do I make a spirit talisman?

Spirit Talismen are woven from greater souls, and drop from the same enemies as those that drop scalps. Frost giants and automatons also produce greater souls.

Weaving spirit talismen is an automatic reaction. If you're butchering many enemies, you may wish to build extra totem poles to help speed their processing.

How do I make ironbone and bloodsteel?

Both of these are made at the totem pole. While any orc can make them, they use the prayer/shaman (aka STRAND_EXTRACTION) labour, which is of particular use to dreamwalkers.
