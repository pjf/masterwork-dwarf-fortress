A few words about weapon usage:

Bare hands, Whips, Flails and Scourges are the favourite weapons of Ghouls. They learn no other weapon skill besides Whip and Wrestling.

Wraithblades and Magestaves are the favourite weapons of Warlocks. They learn no other weapon skill besides Magic-Weapon User. ///they actually have pike-skill as well, but thats only for the graphics. You dont have access to pike weapons.

Swords, Hammers, Axes, Spears, Knives, Bows; all the normal weaponry is used by Skeletons. They can't learn Whip, Wrestling or Magic-Weapon User.

Clothing

Quite normal clothing for everyone. Warlocks can enchant robes with special effects, which only affect Warlocks.

Skeletons won't mind wearing no clothes, but they will still put them on if any are available. Ghouls actually do get a tiny bit sad about having none. Warlocks certainly want clothes.
