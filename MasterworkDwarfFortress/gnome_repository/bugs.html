IMPORTANT: Gnomes REQUIRE the following dfhack scripts for many of their unique behaviors: construct-creature.lua, druidism.lua, geology.lua, and machina.lua. These scripts must also be active in the dfhack.init file. Make sure these files are included and dfhack is running.

 
Automatic machines aren't working.
Bug:
The first constructed automatic machine (Aerial Transportation Device, Autocrafters, etc.) doesn't work when starting a new game or after all automatic machines are deconstructed.
Solution:
Simply designate a new workshop to be built (you can cancel it afterwards). This resets the automatic machine system, which should fix most bugs.

 
Logic gate feedback spiral (Severe machine-related slow-down).
Bug:
The framerate suddenly slows to a crawl when working with machines.
Solution:
Most machines are pretty easy on the framerate, but the entire system must recheck its connections and power levels every time a gear assembly is engaged or disengaged, and power levels must be recalculated if a power source starts or stops. Because of this, if a logic gate is connected to a gear assembly that is connected to one of the logic gate's own input machines, a feedback loop may result that causes the gear assembly to switch on and off every frame, which can kill your framerate. So, avoid this.
Slowdown can also occur if a power source (generally a water wheel whose artificial water source is starting to dry up) is switching on and off rapidly, especially if you have many machines operating. Using natural rivers or steam engines/clockwork keys as your main power source is the best option.

 
Game hangs for several minutes while training animals in bulk
Bug:
The game will freeze, sometimes for as long as several minutes, before resuming as if nothing had happened. This seems to be associated with getting animals to follow each other and pasturing them together in large groups for training.
Solution:
I'm not really sure what causes this. If the game seems to freeze but isn't actually crashing, press the space button so that it pauses the moment it unfreezes. If you have a bunch of animals together in a pasture, remove them from the pasture and then unpause (if you do not, it will probably happen again in a few seconds). This seems to be a DF pathing bug, rather than a direct effect of the script, so it is unlikely that I can fix it without simply removing the option of getting animals to cluster together altogether. In general, try and avoid stuffing too many animals into one small area, or make sure to 'dismiss' them from following each other before you do.

 
Items or creatures launched into the air don't come down.
Bug:
Items or creatures launched at high angles with the Aerial Transport Device sometimes get stuck on invisible platforms in the sky. Not actually an effect of the mod, this is just a quirk of DF topography.
Solution:
Avoid launching items at high angles on maps where this happens. If something's already stuck up there, you'll have to build a stairway to get it down.

 
Tamed birds don't come down.
Bug:
If you tame a bird in mid-flight, it won't path to the ground.
Solution:
Build a stairway up to it to help it find its way.

 
Crash when using autocrafters.

The game crashes while autocrafters are running.

This can happen if an item is made out of an invalid material, or if an invalid item is used in a reaction. Try and figure out what kind of item is causing the problem, and report it in the main forum thread so that it can be made impossible to produce.

Tamed animals reverting to their wild state just hang around.
Bug:
Creatures tamed by the Nature Shrine and reverting to their wild state just follow their tamer around and pick fights with pets, refusing to leave.
Solution:
If you don't want to kill them, try to lure them into a cage trap so they can be re-tamed the normal way. This may require webs or knocking them unconscious. Make sure to designate a (regular) animal training area and set an animal trainer for your pets so this doesn't happen in the first place.

 
Letters are missing, lines are black, no text.
Bug:
Parts of words are missing, letters flicker in and out of existence, long names disappear. A known Vanilla DF TrueTypeFont Bug.
Solution:
No solution, this is hardcoded. You can press F11x2 to fix it temporarily, OR press F12 to turn of TrueType. TrueType is required for the custom Font to work.

 
Workshop reactions are red, even with all the materials available / Where do I get 'rock' trap components?
Bug:
If a workshop is in a burrow, even if the burrow is inactive, then it accepts only materials within the same burrow. 'Rock' is simply DF lingo for rock or metal; if you have a metal trap component and a reaction/building isn't accepting it, burrows are probably your issue.
Solution:
Make a bigger burrow including the materials, or keep the material stockpiles within the same burrow. Or delete the burrow.

 
I have many weird boulders lying around with odd names.
Bug:
Many things in the mod depend on temperature. Things that should melt or boil away don't without it.
Solution:
Open the GUI or Init.txt and set Temperature to ON. This can be done in a running fortress, you only have to restart DF. You do not need to gen a new world. You can also turn off "smoke effects" in the misc features section. This will decrease the amount of these boulders significantly.

I have never been able to replicate this. It appears to be at random, with major differences in the PC system people use. Try shorter histories, <100 years. If the problem persists, please leave a detailed report in the forum. Please mention the mod version, tileset used, if settings have been changed, and how much virtual memory/ram you have.

 
I have an error log.
Bug:

The Dwarf Fortress main folder shows a errorlog.txt. This is created when certain entry in the raws are missing. In 90% of the cases this is completely harmless. It is caused by enabling some settings, and disabling other.

For example you remove warpstone. The error log will tell you that warpstone is missing in the reactions for warpstone landmines and turrets.
Solution:
The default setting has no error log. If your setting has a very big error log, please leave me a message in the forum. Otherwise just ignore it, it will do no harm.
