Plant-Table: All Plants

The optional Setting "More plant types" will add all plants of the flora and fauna mod. A lot. See a list of all plants with features here.

GrowDuration: 1 Season = 1008, 2 Seasons = 2016, 3 Seasons = 3024, 4 Seasons = 4032.

Trees for treefarming grow very slowly. 4 seasons for all trees. Trees can be planted and harvested all year.

WARNING: The information in the tables below is for the vanilla df setting. If you play with "Harder Farming" on, these numbers change. All aboveground plants will need 2 seasons to grow, and should only be planted in spring or summer; and harvested in summer or autumn. If you plant in autumn, you will loose your seeds.

All underground plants will need 4 seasons to grow; and can be planted and harvested the entire year.
